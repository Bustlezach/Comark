About Comark

Comark is a revolutionary online marketplace that connects local sellers with buyers, offering a convenient and efficient way to buy and sell products within your community. Our platform is designed to bring people together, making it easy to discover unique items, support local businesses, and build stronger local connections.
Key Features

Local Product Discovery


Comark allows users to discover a wide range of products available in their local area. Whether you're searching for vintage furniture, handmade crafts, or everyday essentials, you'll find it on our platform. Say goodbye to long shipping times and hello to immediate access to local treasures.

User-Friendly Interface


We prioritize user experience, offering an intuitive and visually appealing interface. Our easy-to-navigate website ensures that both buyers and sellers can effortlessly browse listings, post products, and manage their accounts.

Seamless User Registration


Signing up with Comark is a breeze. As a registered user, you can create your personalized profile, manage your listings, and access advanced features. Our registration process prioritizes data security and user privacy.

Product Listing and Management


Sellers can easily create product listings with essential details, including product name, category, price, description, and location. You have complete control over your listings, allowing you to edit or remove them as needed.

Location-Based Search


Finding products in your neighborhood has never been easier. Our location-based search functionality enables you to search for items near your current location or any specified area, ensuring you can shop locally.

User Profiles


Each user has their dedicated profile page, showcasing their listed items. It's a great way to get to know your local sellers and explore their offerings. Buyers can connect with sellers, ask questions, and make purchases with confidence.

Feedback and Reviews


Transparency and trust are at the core of Comark. Buyers can leave reviews and ratings for sellers, helping build a trusted community of buyers and sellers. Quality and reliability are paramount.

Secure Transactions


We take the security of your transactions seriously. Comark provides a secure platform for payments, ensuring both buyers and sellers have peace of mind when conducting transactions.

Technology Stack>br> Comark is built using a robust technology stack, ensuring a seamless and secure experience for our users. Our technology includes:

Flask: A lightweight and powerful Python web framework that forms the backbone of our application.

SQLAlchemy: An Object-Relational Mapping (ORM) library for Python, used for interacting with our database.

SQLite: A reliable and efficient database management system that stores user and product data.

Jinja2: A template engine for Python, used for rendering dynamic HTML templates.

Flask-Bcrypt: A Flask extension for secure password hashing and storage.

Flask-Login: A Flask extension for user session management and authentication.

Geolocation API: We use a reverse geocoding API to provide location-based search functionality, ensuring users can find products in their vicinity.

HTML, CSS, and JavaScript: The frontend is designed using HTML, styled with CSS, and enhanced with JavaScript to deliver a responsive and user-friendly interface.
